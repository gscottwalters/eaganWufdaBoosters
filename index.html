Our Mission

The WUFDA Ultimate Boosters will provide essential support to the program through organization, communication, and fundraising. Their efforts will help cover costs for equipment, tournament fees, travel, and team events, ensuring that every athlete has the resources to succeed. The boosters will also coordinate volunteer opportunities, promote the program in the community, and strengthen connections among players, families, and supporters. By building a strong foundation of financial and organizational backing, the booster group will allow coaches and athletes to focus on growth, competition, and team spirit.
