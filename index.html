Our Mission

The Eagan Ultimate Boosters help keep the program running strong by managing key administrative tasks, handling communication, organizing volunteers, and raising funds for coaching, fields, equipment, and tournament costs. We also promote the program and support team activities. Eagan Ultimate is powered by the Eagan Athletic Association (EAA). The boosters are led by a four-member board—the President, who represents Eagan Ultimate on the EAA Board and is responsible for overall program administration, the Treasurer, the Communications Director, and the Coaching Director—with additional Committee Chairs overseeing areas such as fundraising and team and tournament support. With this structure in place, coaches and athletes can focus on developing as players, competing, and fostering the Spirit of the Game.

Volunteer

Parents and supporters are essential to the success of WUFDA. As our program grows, we rely on Booster leadership and parent volunteers to create the best possible experience for every athlete. Making each season happen truly takes a village: Your time and energy directly support program sustainability, league and tournament participation, travel, team events and community building. No prior experience is required, just a willingness to help our athletes and contribute to a fun, inclusive Ultimate community.

Communcation and Publicity: Help keep our community informed and connected by supporting tasks such as social media updates, player photos, yearbook content, senior recognition, recruitment events, and sharing news with EHS communications.

Fundraising: Support the essential costs of equipment, tournaments, and team events by assisting with new fundraising opportunities, supporting Cub bagging or dine-out nights, managing donations or pursuing grants.

Team & Tournament Support: Help shape a great season experience by coordinating tournament volunteers, hosting team-building activities or pre-tournament meals, provisioning tournament snacks, planning end-of-season events, or arranging coaches’ gifts or spirit prizes.

Your involvement keeps Eagan Ultimate strong and ensures a great experience for all athletes!
