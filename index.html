Our Mission

The Eagan Ultimate Boosters supports program operations, providing organizational, financial, and volunteer assistance that enables coaches and athletes to focus on player development, competition, and the Spirit of the Game.

Volunteer

Eagan Ultimate relies on volunteers to support the day-to-day operations and seasonal activities that keep the program running smoothly. Examples of ways to get involved include supporting tournament logistics, organizing team events, coordinating senior recognition, helping with fundraising, taking game-day photos, organizing yearbook content, arranging spirit prizes or end of season gifts, assisting with general administrative needs or even volunteering to join the Boosters Board. Please complete the volunteer form to let us know how you can support the program!
